About Who We Are and What We Do

Since 1966, our school has been dedicated to championing the rights of the child by providing an exceptional Montessori education in the Philippines.

We are deeply committed to the philosophy and educational method developed by Dr. Maria Montessori.

We have 5 campuses that offer accessible, high-quality Montessori education in a nurturing environment for learning, creativity, and development.

The Generations We've Nurtured

O.B. Montessori Center (OBMC) has educated generations of students since its founding in 1966.

OBMC has been raising and developing generations of global citizens committed to service and the cause of peace.

Our alumni are a testament to the transformative power of the Montessori education.

Our Legacy

The "OBMC legacy" refers to the long-standing mission of the Operation Brotherhood Montessori Center in the Philippines.

The legacy is continued through its successful alumni who have become leaders and contributing forces in various fields.

OBMC continues its mission of empowering young minds and nurturing future leaders.
